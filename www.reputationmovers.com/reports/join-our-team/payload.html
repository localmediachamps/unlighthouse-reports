Full Time

Driver

$17.00 - $22.00 an hour depending on experience

Reputation Movers is looking for quality individuals to join our team as moving truck drivers. We are seeking physically fit, responsible, and reliable team members to carefully wrap and transport items into and out of moving trucks and homes. We are team-oriented, hardworking, and only want to hire great people to work with who will bring a good attitude to all jobs. We believe communication is key to making this an awesome place to work and we work hard to make sure that this is a great place to work for everyone.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Must have a current, valid, driver's license and a clean driving record.
  • Secure all items and furniture in the proper protective material on every job. Load, pack and unload items with care.
  • Disassemble and reassemble all items of furniture that require it for a safe move.
  • Ensure that no items are damaged while moving.
  • Communicate effectively with the team, clients, and managers on every job and while in the warehouse. This is a HEAVY customer service-oriented job and rudeness to our team or customers will not be tolerated.
  • Must have prior moving or warehousing experience.
  • No CDL required (but is a major plus) just a valid driver's license, a clean driving record, some moving or warehouse experience, and the desire to learn.
  • You will be required to drive 26-foot trucks along with the responsibilities stated above. Training will be provided, but you must be willing to learn and/or have prior experience.

Benefits:

  • Dental insurance
  • Flexible schedule
  • Health insurance
  • Life insurance
  • Paid time off
  • Vision insurance

Supplemental pay types:

  • Tips

We have a fantastic reputation among our clients and they have always taken care of our team. Tips are given on most jobs and range from anywhere between $20 - $100+.

After 3-6 months on our team, we offer paid vacation, health insurance, dental insurance, paid holidays, and 90-day reviews. Show us that you work hard and want to grow with the team and we will make sure to take care of you.

We have a warehouse located in Alexandria, VA where we dispatch from for all jobs, a 10-minute walk from the Eisenhower Metro. Coffee, water, and snacks are provided daily.
