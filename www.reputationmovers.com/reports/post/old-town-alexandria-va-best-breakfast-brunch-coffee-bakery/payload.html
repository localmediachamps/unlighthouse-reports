Best Breakfast in Old Town Alexandria, VA

King Street Café

King Street Café is a cozy breakfast and lunch spot located in the heart of Old Town Alexandria. Known for its friendly service and fresh, made-to-order sandwiches, it has become a favorite among locals and visitors alike. The café offers a welcoming atmosphere where patrons can enjoy a variety of classic American dishes.

  • Address: 1018 King St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Monday to Saturday: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • Price Range: $8 – $12 per entrée
  • Signature Dish: Breakfast Burritos, Western Omelette, Steak and Cheese Sub, California Club Wrap
  • Parking: Metered street parking is available along King Street. Additionally, there is a small metered parking lot nearby at 920 King Street.
  • Peak Times: Weekday mornings between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM are typically the busiest.
  • Takeout: Available for both breakfast and lunch;

Royal Restaurant

As one of Alexandria’s oldest establishments, Royal Restaurant has been serving traditional American breakfasts since 1904. This historic gem offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making it a favorite among locals and tourists. With its affordable prices and famous breakfast buffet, it’s an excellent choice for a satisfying meal.

  • Address: 734 N Saint Asaph St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Daily, 6:30 AM to 3:00 PM
  • Price Range: $8-15 per entrée
  • Signature Dish: Breakfast buffet with made-to-order omelets and Belgian waffles
  • Parking: Municipal parking available across the street
  • Peak Times: 8:00-9:30 AM on weekdays
  • Takeout: Available through a dedicated counter

Table Talk

Located in a renovated Victorian townhouse, Table Talk provides a sophisticated breakfast experience with a touch of elegance. The restaurant is celebrated for its innovative spins on breakfast classics, making it a standout option in Old Town. Using organic ingredients and partnering with local farmers, they deliver fresh and flavorful dishes.

  • Address: 1623 Duke St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 2:00 PM
  • Price Range: $15-25 per entrée
  • Signature Dish: Truffle scrambled eggs and house-cured salmon on fresh bagels
  • Parking: Valet parking during peak hours; street parking nearby
  • Peak Times: 8:30-10:00 AM on weekdays
  • Takeout: Premium service with insulated packaging

Magnolia's on King

Magnolia’s on King offers a Southern-inspired breakfast with warm hospitality and bold flavors. Housed in a historic building, this cozy restaurant serves dishes that highlight seasonal and locally sourced ingredients. It’s a wonderful spot to enjoy comfort food with a contemporary twist.

  • Address: 703 King St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Tuesday through Sunday, 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM
  • Price Range: $12-22 per entrée
  • Signature Dish: Sweet potato pancakes and shrimp and grits
  • Parking: Public parking garage one block away; limited street parking
  • Peak Times: 9:00-11:00 AM on weekends
  • Takeout: Online ordering available

Best Brunch in Old Town Alexandria, VA

Virtue Feed & Grain

Located in a beautifully restored 19th-century feed house, Virtue Feed & Grain offers a refined brunch experience in a rustic setting. Known for its fresh seafood and creative takes on classic dishes, this spot is perfect for a leisurely weekend meal. The upscale ambiance and commitment to locally sourced ingredients make it a must-visit destination in Old Town.

  • Address: 106 S Union St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Saturday and Sunday, 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM
  • Price Range: $18-30 per entrée
  • Signature Dish: Crab cake Benedict and bourbon French toast
  • Parking: Valet parking on weekends; garage parking nearby
  • Peak Times: 11:00 AM-1:00 PM
  • Takeout: Streamlined service available

Fontaine Caffe & Creperie

Fontaine Caffe & Crêperie is a charming eatery nestled in Old Town Alexandria, offering a delightful fusion of French cuisine and local flavors. Known for its savory and sweet crêpes, the café provides a cozy atmosphere reminiscent of a Parisian bistro. With a commitment to fresh, local, and organic ingredients, Fontaine ensures a memorable dining experience for all guests.

  • Address: 119 S. Royal Street, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Monday – Friday: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM; 5:30 PM – 10:00 PM; Saturday – Sunday: 9:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  • Price Range: $15-25 per entrée
  • Signature Dish: Crepes and Bruschetta
  • Parking: Metered street parking is available along S. Royal Street and adjacent areas. Additionally, public parking garages are located within walking distance.
  • Peak Times: Weekends from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM are typically the busiest, especially during brunch hours.
  • Takeout: Available for all menu items; orders can be placed in person or by phone.

Union Street Public House

Set in a charming colonial warehouse, Union Street Public House offers a blend of historical ambiance and modern brunch options. Their menu showcases sustainable sourcing and seasonal specials, ensuring fresh flavors year-round. It’s a fantastic spot for brunch with a side of history.

  • Address: 121 S Union St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Saturday and Sunday, 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM
  • Price Range: $16-28 per entrée
  • Signature Dish: Seafood frittata and bourbon pecan French toast
  • Parking: Street parking and public garages within walking distance
  • Peak Times: 12:00 PM-2:00 PM on Sundays
  • Takeout: Available through delivery platforms

Best Coffee Shops in Old Town Alexandria, VA

Misha's Coffee Roaster & Coffee House

A cornerstone of Old Town’s coffee culture, Misha’s has been roasting and serving top-quality coffee since 1991. With its warm and inviting atmosphere, it’s a favorite spot for a morning pick-me-up or a leisurely cup of coffee. They take pride in their expertly roasted beans and partnerships with local bakeries.

  • Address: 102 S Patrick St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Monday through Sunday, 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM
  • Price Range: $3-7 for beverages
  • Signature Item: Rotating single-origin selections and signature blends
  • Parking: Two-hour street parking; validated garage parking nearby
  • Peak Times: 7:30-9:00 AM on weekdays
  • Takeout: Mobile ordering available

ESP Tea & Coffee

Located in a historic 19th-century building, ESP Tea & Coffee offers a unique blend of high-quality coffee and artisanal teas. With its cozy interior and premium ingredients, it’s a great place to relax and unwind. Their creative menu features both classic beverages and specialty drinks with a twist.

  • Address: 1012 King St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Monday through Saturday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM; Sunday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM
  • Price Range: $3.50-8 for beverages
  • Signature Item: Old Town Fog (lavender Earl Grey latte) and single-origin pour-overs
  • Parking: Metered street parking; public garage three blocks away
  • Peak Times: 8:00-10:00 AM on weekdays
  • Takeout: Online ordering available

For Five Coffee Roasters

For Five Coffee Roasters brings New York-style coffee culture to Old Town Alexandria with its sleek design and exceptional menu. This modern coffee shop is known for its high-quality beverages and seasonal offerings. Whether you’re grabbing a quick drink or staying for breakfast, it’s a top choice.

  • Address: 715 King St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Daily, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM
  • Price Range: $4-8 for beverages
  • Signature Item: Maple Butter Latte and house-made cold brew
  • Parking: Validated parking program at adjacent garage
  • Peak Times: 7:30-9:30 AM weekdays; weekend brunches
  • Takeout: Advance ordering via their app

St. Elmo's Coffee Pub

A beloved neighborhood institution, St. Elmo’s Coffee Pub has been a gathering place for locals for decades. Known for its community-focused events and quality coffee, this shop combines great drinks with a welcoming environment. Their housemade pastries are also a standout.

  • Address: 2300 Mount Vernon Ave, Alexandria, VA 22301
  • Hours: Monday through Friday, 6:30 AM to 8:00 PM; weekends, 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM
  • Price Range: $3-6 for beverages
  • Signature Item: Carefully crafted espresso drinks
  • Parking: Street parking available; spots behind the building
  • Peak Times: 7:00-9:00 AM on weekdays
  • Takeout: Online and phone ordering available

Swing's Coffee Roasters

With a legacy dating back to 1916, Swing’s Coffee Roasters blends historical significance with exceptional coffee. Their meticulous roasting process and strong partnerships with coffee farmers set them apart. This shop is ideal for coffee purists and history enthusiasts alike.

  • Address: 501 E Monroe Ave, Alexandria, VA 22301
  • Hours: Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM; weekends, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  • Price Range: $3.50-7 for beverages
  • Signature Item: Single-origin coffees and traditional espresso drinks
  • Parking: Two-hour street parking; public lot one block away
  • Peak Times: 8:00-10:00 AM weekdays
  • Takeout: Website and app-based ordering

Best Bakeries in Old Town Alexandria, VA

Alexandria Pastry Shop

Established in 1988, Alexandria Pastry Shop is a cornerstone of the Old Town bakery scene. This European-inspired bakery offers an extensive selection of pastries, breads, and desserts made with locally sourced ingredients. Whether you’re in the mood for a flaky croissant or a decadent tart, this spot is sure to satisfy.

  • Address: 3690 King St, Alexandria, VA 22302
  • Hours: Tuesday through Sunday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM
  • Price Range: $3-8 per item
  • Signature Items: Almond croissants and Old Town cherry tarts
  • Parking: Dedicated parking lot available
  • Peak Times: 8:00-10:00 AM on weekends
  • Takeout: Advance orders accepted through their website

Le Pain Quotidien

Part of a respected international chain, Le Pain Quotidien offers a taste of Belgium in Old Town. This upscale bakery is known for its beautifully presented baked goods and emphasis on organic ingredients. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick coffee and pastry or a leisurely breakfast, it’s a reliable choice.

  • Address: 701 King St, Alexandria, VA 22314
  • Hours: Daily, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM
  • Price Range: $4-12 per item
  • Signature Items: Classic sourdough bread and Belgian pastries
  • Parking: Metered street parking; public garage one block away
  • Peak Times: 8:30-10:30 AM on weekends
  • Takeout: Available through their website

Whether you’re craving a hearty breakfast, creative brunch, expertly brewed coffee, or freshly baked treats, this guide has something for every taste and occasion. Explore these spots and discover why Old Town is a beloved destination for food lovers.
